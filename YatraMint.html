DUBAI

Dubai is a city and emirate in the United Arab Emirates known for luxury shopping, ultramodern architecture and a lively nightlife scene. Burj Khalifa, an 830m-tall tower, dominates the skyscraper-filled skyline. At its foot lies Dubai Fountain, with jets and lights choreographed to music. On artificial islands just offshore is Atlantis, The Palm, a resort with water and marine-animal parks

THAILAND

The primary city in Thailand is Bangkok (Krung Thep Maha Nakhon), the bustling capital known for its ornate temples, vibrant street life, amazing food, shopping, and rich culture, serving as Thailand's economic and political heart. Other major cities include Chiang Mai, famous for its ancient temples, handicrafts, and cooler northern atmosphere, and tourist hubs like Phuket and Pattaya, known for their stunning beaches and entertainment.
